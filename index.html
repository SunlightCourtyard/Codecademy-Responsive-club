About Us

The Stellar Seekers club is dedicated to fostering a community of space enthusiasts and promoting the exploration and understanding of the cosmos. Our mission is to inspire curiosity, spark wonder, and cultivate a deeper appreciation for the wonders of the universe among people of all ages and backgrounds. Through education, outreach, and hands-on experiences, we aim to ignite a passion for space science and exploration, empowering individuals to explore the mysteries of the cosmos and contribute to humanity's collective journey beyond Earth.

  1. Education and Outreach: To provide accessible and engaging educational resources, events, and activities that promote space science literacy and foster a greater understanding of the universe.
  2. Stargazing and Observation: To organize regular stargazing outings and observation sessions, providing members with opportunities to explore the night sky, observe celestial phenomena, and learn about the wonders of the cosmos firsthand.
  3. Community Building: To cultivate a supportive and inclusive community of space enthusiasts, where members can share their passion for astronomy, astrophysics, and space exploration and collaborate on projects and initiatives.
  4. Inspiration and Exploration: To inspire curiosity and wonder about the universe and encourage members to explore new frontiers in space science and exploration through discussions, presentations, and hands-on activities.
  5. Advocacy and Impact: To advocate for the importance of space exploration and scientific research and empower members to make a positive impact in their communities by sharing their knowledge, enthusiasm, and passion for space science.

The Stellar Seekers club was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate space enthusiasts who shared a common desire to explore the mysteries of the cosmos and share their fascination with others. What began as a small group of friends gathering to observe the night sky quickly grew into a vibrant community of like-minded individuals united by their love for astronomy, astrophysics, and space exploration. Over the years, the club has organized numerous events, workshops, and stargazing outings, bringing together people from all walks of life to learn, discover, and explore the wonders of the universe. From observing meteor showers and eclipses to hosting guest speakers and participating in outreach activities, the Stellar Seekers have remained committed to their mission of inspiring curiosity and fostering a deeper appreciation for the cosmos. As the club continues to grow and evolve, we remain dedicated to our founding principles of education, outreach, and community building, and we look forward to embarking on new adventures and discoveries together as we journey among the stars.

Events

Stellar Night Sky Viewing

  • Date: Saturday, May 11th, 2024
  • Time: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Location: Stellar Observatory, Houston

Join us for an evening of stargazing at our observatory located just outside Houston. Experienced astronomers will guide participants in observing celestial wonders such as planets, stars, and galaxies through telescopes. Learn about the constellations and enjoy the beauty of the night sky.

Space Science Workshop: Exploring Mars

  • Date: Saturday, June 15th, 2024
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Location: Houston Space Center, Discovery Hall

Dive into the fascinating world of Mars exploration at our interactive workshop hosted at the Houston Space Center. Discover the latest discoveries about the Red Planet, learn about past and present Mars missions, and participate in hands-on activities to simulate Martian exploration.

Meteor Shower Watch Party

  • Date: Friday, August 9th, 2024
  • Time: 11:00 PM - 3:00 AM (Next Day)
  • Location: Dark Sky Reserve, Near Houston

Join us for a night under the stars as we witness the annual Perseid meteor shower. We'll gather at a designated dark sky reserve just outside of Houston to observe the spectacular meteor shower away from city lights. Bring your blankets, telescopes, and snacks for an unforgettable celestial experience.

Space Science Symposium: Beyond the Solar System

  • Date: Saturday, December 14th, 2024
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Location: Houston University, Astronomy Department

Expand your horizons at our space science symposium focused on exploring the mysteries of the universe beyond our solar system. Join leading astronomers and astrophysicists for a day of informative talks, panel discussions, and Q&A sessions covering topics such as exoplanets, black holes, and the search for extraterrestrial life.
